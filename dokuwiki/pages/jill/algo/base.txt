====== Base background ======

Base background is animate background (5 pictures).\\
Background is update at each cycle.\\

To draw border, you need check background next to, to draw right mask.

When back is alone, you need apply mask tileset 42, tile 5.\\
When back is right top corner, you need apply mask tileset 42, tile 1.\\
When back is left top corner, you need apply mask tileset 42, tile 2.\\
When back is right bottom corner, you need apply mask tileset 42, tile 3.\\
When back is left bottom corner, you need apply mask tileset 42, tile 4.\\
