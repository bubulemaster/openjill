====== Bubble ======

Bubbles are created by Underwatter rock.

Bubble move X random between -1/0/1. Becarefull. Bubble move directly. //iXD// is not set.

^ iCounter ^ tileset ^ tile ^ Move Y ^
| 0 | 51 | 6 | -1 |
| 1 | 51 | 7 | -2 |
| 2 | 51 | 8 | -3 |

Becarefull. Bubble move directly. //iYD// is not set.

Randomly, when move X is 0, bubble change //iCounter// to increase +1.
